TYPOGRAPHY:

- Primary blue: Use for key numbers (payment, amount financed)
- Dark: Most text and values
- Muted: Labels and helper text

- Yellow/Orange: Warnings (over budget, missing info)
- Red: Errors or critical actions

- Between sections: 24-32px
- Between fields: 16-20px
- Card padding: 16-20px
- Generous throughout (no cramped feeling)

- Selected items: Border + shadow + background tint
- Modals: Strong shadow (20-30px blur)

- Cards: 12-16px border radius
- Buttons: 8-12px border radius
- Inputs: 8px border radius
- Pills/badges: Fully rounded (999px)

ANIMATIONS:
- All transitions: 200-300ms ease
- Tab switches: Smooth fade
- Payment recalculation: Brief pulse/glow
- Saves: Success checkmark animation
- Keep subtle - not distracting
